About

Hello!

Wellcome to the Games Development Society! Whether you are an experienced developer/designer, an artist or writer, a sound designer or simply just interested in games, you are in the right place! You don't need to know anything beforehand, our society is a great place to start learning or further develop the skills you already have.

Regularly, we do weekly meetings where we hold tutorials, play and make games. We also do weekly challenges where you get a problem of design/development and have one week to solve. Monthly we'll hold game jams! See more here

If you need anything, have ideas, suggestions or any issues, please don't hesitate to contact us!
